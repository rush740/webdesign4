About Club

Founded in the early 1960's. Early members and founders include Eddie Byrnes, Jim Nugent,
Tom Martin, Paddy O' Donnell and David Keane.
Current membership is fifty seven, all members are required to live within club boundaries.
We employ various conservation methods to maintain healthy
animal and bird populations and the future sustainability of the club.
All members are required to have appropriate safety training this can be arranged by the club.
